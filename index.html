Base Rent: This is the rent amount that is charged for the apartment without any additional fees or charges.
Square Footage: This refers to the total area of the apartment measured in square feet.
Security Deposit: This is a refundable amount of money that is paid by the tenant to the landlord at the start of the lease. It is held as a guarantee against any damage or unpaid rent that may occur during the lease period.
Move-In Costs: This includes all the costs associated with moving into the apartment, such as the first month's rent, security deposit, and any fees charged by the landlord or property management company.
Utilities: This refers to the basic services provided to the apartment, such as electricity, gas, water, and internet. Some apartments include these costs in the rent, while others require tenants to pay for them separately.
Application Fee: This is a non-refundable fee charged by the landlord or property management company for processing the tenant's rental application.
